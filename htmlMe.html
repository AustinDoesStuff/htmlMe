Want to get involved in the revolution?

Get involved the Campaign:

There are many ways you can get involved with the campaign. One of the easiest and least invasive is phone banking or canvasing. You can also get involved by looking out for local events or even hosting some yourself.

Register to vote, then vote:

Registering to vote is the easy part, the hard part is to get Grimace on the ballet you have to check if your state has an open or closed primary.

An open primary is easy, all you have to do is be registered to vote and you can walk in and vote.

A closed primary is hard, however, because you have to be a registered Democrat by a specific date or you can't vote in the primary. So check your state's local election and get Grimace elected!
