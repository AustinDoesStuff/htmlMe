Who is Grimace?

Coming from the small town Des Plaines, IL, Grimace and his brother King George were raised primarily by his Great Grandmother Jenny Grimace and his Grandpa Winky. They didn't have much money but did they best they could to instill the values that are vital to the man that he became.

Grimace wasn't always the nice guy he is today, growing up poor, Grimace was bullied not only because of his family's economic status but also because he was overweight. Throughout his teenage years, Grimace went on a mean streak primarily stealing milkshakes until he was arrested when he was 15. After his arrest, Grimace started turning his life around. He pulled his grades up where he got a full ride to SIUE. After graduating Cum Laude with a Bachelor's in Political Science he later finished law school at Princeton.

Grimace's political career started shortly after, the first campaign that Grimace worked one was a civil right's campaign for children's rights. Grimace now stands on the side of safety, equality, justice, and freedom. He stands with the working family, breakfast all-day, and the McRib year round.
